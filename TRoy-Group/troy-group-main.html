About TRoy Group™

Founded by Ertan (Founder & CEO), TRoy Group™ represents a deliberate evolution from traditional regional shipping and travel agencies into a fully digital, internationally-scoped business ecosystem. With deep expertise in Turkish maritime operations, Turkish Straits logistics, and 30+ years of industry experience, we've transformed our operations into cutting-edge, online-first ventures.

Today, we operate three distinct yet complementary divisions—Maritime, Travel, and Trading—each leveraging digital innovation and professional expertise to serve clients worldwide.

🚢 Maritime Heritage
