About TRoy Group™

Founded by Ertan Göçer (Founder & CEO), TRoy Group™ is a multi-division international business umbrella operating 100% online. With deep expertise in Turkish maritime operations, Turkish Straits logistics, and 30+ years of industry experience, TRoy Group™ delivers professional, precise, and globally connected services across three specialised divisions.

TRoyMAR™ (Maritime), TRoyGo™ (Travel), and TRoyTR™ (Trading) — each division brings specialist expertise, unified under a single group philosophy: Precise. Honest. Professional.
