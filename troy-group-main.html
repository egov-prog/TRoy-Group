About TRoy Group™

TRoy Group™ is a multi-division international business umbrella operating 100% online, serving clients and partners worldwide. Our three divisions — TRoy Maritime Agency™, TRoyGo™ (Travel), and TRoyTR™ (Trading) — each bring specialist expertise to their respective sectors, unified under a single group philosophy: Precise. Honest. Professional.

The group was founded by Ertan Göçer, bringing over 30 years of hands-on experience in the maritime industry — including specialist knowledge of the Turkish Straits (Çanakkale/Dardanelles and Bosphorus), one of the world's most strategically important maritime chokepoints. This deep operational background extends across all three divisions, informing how TRoy Group™ approaches international commerce, logistics, and trade.
