About TRoy Group™

30+ Years of Expertise.
Built for the Digital Age.

TRoy Group™ is an international business umbrella founded by Ertan Govdeli, operating 100% online from Darwin, Northern Territory, Australia.

The group brings together four specialist divisions — maritime, travel, trading and media — each operating independently online with a shared commitment to digital innovation, transparency and world-class service.

With roots in the Turkish Straits region (Çanakkale/Dardanelles), TRoy Group™ is built on deep maritime expertise and a 30+ year career spanning ship agency, travel and international trade.
